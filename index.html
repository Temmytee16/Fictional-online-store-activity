Welcome to Oli sporting

Oli sporting was created by Temitope Olikuntuyi in September 2025. he created Oli sporting right after he retired from U.S Navy after serving 6 years. He was motivated to create this brand after seeing the military had no sport brand of clothing.

Our Mission

Oli sporting mission is to ensure all military athletes are well equipped and have high-quality sportswear to function properly and help with their performance on and off the field

Our Vision

Oli sporting vision is to take military athletes to a greater stage and ensure they are ready for anything coming their way, and to also be known world wide
